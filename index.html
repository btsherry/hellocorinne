Hello Corinne

For Corinne

In all the noise of spinning worlds, you are the quiet I come home to — the steady light across the water, the voice that makes the long week worth it. You are Vashon on a Friday evening, the exhale after every held breath, the reason the ferry feels like flying no matter how tired I am when I board. I would choose this life, and you in it, a thousand times and counting still. Every version of the story ends with me beside you.
